3 Months Warranty

We stand behind our work. Every repair service provided by 24x7 Service Store comes with a standard 90-Day (3 Months) Warranty on spare parts and service labor.

Warranty Coverage Details

  • ✅ Spare Parts: If a replaced part fails within 90 days, we replace it FREE of cost.
  • ✅ Service Labor: No service charge if the same issue reoccurs within the warranty period.
  • ✅ Hassle-Free Claims: Just call our support number, and we'll prioritize your warranty claim.

*Warranty is applicable only on the specific parts replaced and the specific issue repaired. Physical damage or voltage fluctuation damage is not covered.

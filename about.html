About 24x7 Service TV Repair

Who We Are

24x7 Service is a premier, independent TV repair service center operating in Lucknow, India. We specialize in repairing all major TV brands including Sony, Samsung, LG, MI, Panasonic, and many others, regardless of warranty status. With over 10 years of experience in the electronics repair industry, we have earned the trust of thousands of satisfied customers through our commitment to quality, reliability, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable TV repair services to the residents of Lucknow. We believe that every customer deserves professional service at their doorstep without the hassle of traveling to service centers. We aim to extend the lifespan of your TVs and provide value-for-money repairs with genuine spare parts.
